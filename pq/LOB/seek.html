#int pq\LOB::seek(int $offset[, int $whence = SEEK_SET])

Params:#

  • int $offset#
    The position to seek to.
  • Optional int $whence# = SEEK_SET
    From where to seek (SEEK_SET, SEEK_CUR or SEEK_END).

  • int, the new position.
